JUDO

Judo is a Japanese martial art that emphasizes techniques like throws, pins, and joint locks. At DoBu, skilled instructors are available to impart the fundamentals and advanced aspects of Judo to students. Engaging in Judo training at DoBu offers numerous advantages, including enhanced physical fitness, improved self-defense abilities, and an overall boost in well-being.

KARATE

Karate, hailing from Okinawa, Japan, focuses on striking techniques involving various body parts, including hands and feet. DoBu provides Karate lessons tailored for individuals of all skill levels, from novices to advanced practitioners. Enrolling in DoBu's Karate program empowers students to boost their self-assurance, instill discipline, enhance physical prowess, and refine coordination and flexibility.

Jiu-jitsu

Jiu-jitsu is a martial discipline that centers on ground-based combat and grappling methods. DoBu provides an opportunity for individuals to acquire Jiu-jitsu expertise under the guidance of seasoned instructors, enabling them to enhance their abilities in self-protection, physical well-being, and mental resilience. The practice of Jiu-jitsu also brings advantages such as better balance, coordination, flexibility, and overall physical health.

MUAY-THAI

Muay Thai, originating from Thailand, is a martial art that centers on striking techniques involving fists, elbows, knees, and shins. DoBu provides Muay Thai lessons suitable for individuals at all skill levels, led by experienced instructors. Enrolling in Muay Thai classes at DoBu empowers students to enhance their physical fitness, coordination, and self-defense proficiency, as well as their mental resilience and discipline.
